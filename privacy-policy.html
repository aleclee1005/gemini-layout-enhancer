Gemini Layout Enhancer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Gemini Layout Enhancer is a browser extension that adds layout enhancement features to Google Gemini. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is used.

Data Collection

Gemini Layout Enhancer does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers. All processing happens entirely within your browser.

Permissions Explained

PermissionWhy it is needed
content_scripts on gemini.google.com Injects layout enhancement buttons (Widescreen, Split View, Focus Mode, Export Markdown) directly into the Gemini interface. No data is read or transmitted.

Compatibility Notice

This extension depends on Google Gemini's DOM structure. If Google updates Gemini's interface, some features may temporarily stop working. Updates will be released as soon as possible when breakage is detected.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.
